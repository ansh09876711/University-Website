Eligibility Criteria & Admission Process

Follow the steps below to complete your admission at EDUSTACK University.

Admission Process

Step 1

Register Yourself and create your account.

Step 2

Verify your Email ID through OTP confirmation.

Step 3

Complete your Profile and fill in the application form.

Step 4

Upload required documents to secure your application.

Step 5

Pay the application fees online.

Step 6

Seat allotment will be based on First Come First Serve basis.

Step 7

Final document verification and fee payment.
