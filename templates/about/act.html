Act, Statutes & Memorandum of Understanding (MoU)

Institutional Governance, Policies & Industry Collaborations

📜 Institutional Act & Statutes

Edustack University operates under academic regulations and statutory framework aligned with AICTE guidelines and RGPV University policies. These statutes define governance structure, academic procedures, student evaluation systems, and institutional administration.

  • Academic governance framework
  • Faculty recruitment policies
  • Student evaluation and grading rules
  • Research and innovation guidelines
  • Institutional ethics and compliance policies

🤝 Memorandum of Understanding (MoU)

The college maintains strong partnerships with industries and academic organizations through Memorandum of Understanding agreements to enhance student learning, research collaboration, and placement opportunities.

  • Industry training collaborations
  • Internship and placement partnerships
  • Research collaboration with institutions
  • Skill development programs
  • Guest lectures and industry mentorship

🏛 Regulatory Compliance

All institutional policies comply with guidelines from AICTE, NAAC, and affiliated university regulations to ensure quality education and transparent governance.

  • AICTE approval compliance
  • NAAC quality standards
  • University affiliation policies
  • Academic audit procedures
